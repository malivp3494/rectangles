Rectangles

✔ Alright, listen up!

✔ This is a game, and to play it, you just click on the play area to get a rectangle of random size. You place it in such a way that it doesn't cross another rectangle's length / width.

✔ If the rectangle crosses another rectangle, Game Over!

✔ You get a point for every rectangle you place.

✔ Don't worry, you get 3 lives every time you start a new game. So, if you think you can't fit the rectangle, just press Esc on your keyboard.
